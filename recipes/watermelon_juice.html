Fresh Watermelon Juice

Description:

A delicious and refreshing juice for the summer

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of watermelon, cubed and seeds removed
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lime juice
  • 1-2 teaspoons of honey or sugar (optional, depending on the sweetness of the watermelon)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the watermelon cubes and lime juice. If desired, add honey or sugar to taste.
  2. Using a blender, puree the watermelon mixture until smooth. If you prefer a chunkier texture, blend less.
  3. If using, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again until the ice is crushed and the juice is chilled.
  4. Pour the watermelon juice into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy your refreshing drink!
